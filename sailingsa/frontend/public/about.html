South African Sailing Results, Regatta Results & Sailor Profiles

SailingSA is South Africa's searchable sailing results database. We provide regatta results, sailor profiles, class standings, and historical race results across Optimist, ILCA (Laser), 420, keelboat and youth sailing events in South Africa.

Search South African Sailing Results By:

  • Individual sailor name (first name or surname)
  • Regatta name (Youth Nationals, Cape Classic, GIMCO, etc.)
  • Class (Optimist, ILCA 4, ILCA 6, ILCA 7, 420, L26, Dabchick)
  • Club (Hermanus Yacht Club, RCYC, Saldanha Bay Yacht Club)
  • Province or region

SailingSA is a searchable database of South African sailing results. Unlike static PDF result pages, SailingSA allows users to search and filter results by sailor, regatta, class or club.

How To Use Search

Use the Sailor tab to search by sailor name or SAS ID. Use the Regatta tab to search by event name. Results include position, fleet, event, and historical performance data.

Public vs Logged In Access

Public users can search sailor profiles and view regatta results. Logged-in users access extended race breakdowns, analytics, and historical comparisons.

Our Coverage

We cover Optimist, ILCA 4, ILCA 6, ILCA 7, 420, Dabchick, Mirror, Hobie 16, L26 keelboats, and major South African national and regional regattas.

Why SailingSA Exists

SailingSA is the searchable sailing results database for South Africa. We built it to provide a centralised, searchable database of South African sailing results. Unlike static PDF archives, SailingSA connects regatta results directly to sailor profiles, making performance data structured, searchable, and transparent.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find sailing results in South Africa?

Use the SailingSA search bar to search by sailor name, regatta name, class or club.

Does SailingSA include Optimist results?

Yes. SailingSA includes Optimist A and B fleet results across South African regattas.

Does SailingSA include ILCA results?

Yes. ILCA 4, ILCA 6 and ILCA 7 results are searchable by sailor and regatta.

Can I view Hermanus Yacht Club results?

Yes. Results from Hermanus Yacht Club and other South African clubs are indexed where available.
