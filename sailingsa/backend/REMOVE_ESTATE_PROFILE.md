# Remove "E Estate" Chrome Profile

## Why It's Still Showing:

The **Google account** (portalestate3@gmail.com) is deleted ✅
But the **Chrome profile** "E Estate" is separate - it's stored locally on your Mac.

## Delete It Manually:

### In Chrome:

1. **Click your profile icon** (top right of Chrome)
2. **Click "Manage Chrome profiles"** (gear icon at bottom)
3. **Find "E Estate"** in the list
4. **Click the three dots (⋮)** next to "E Estate"
5. **Click "Delete"**
6. **Confirm deletion**

### Or Via Chrome Settings:

1. **Chrome menu** → **Settings** (or Cmd+,)
2. **Click "Manage Chrome profiles"** on left
3. **Find "E Estate"**
4. **Click three dots (⋮)** → **Delete**

## After Deletion:

The "E Estate" profile will disappear from Chrome's profile menu.

## Note:

- Chrome profiles are just local browser data
- Safe to delete - won't affect anything
- Google account is already deleted ✅
- This is just cleaning up the local Chrome profile
