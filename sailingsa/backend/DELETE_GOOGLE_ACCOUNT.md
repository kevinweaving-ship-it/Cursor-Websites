# How to Delete Google Account

## Steps to Delete:

1. **Go to Data and Privacy** (I've opened this page for you)
   - Or manually: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy

2. **Scroll down** to find "Data from apps and services you use"

3. **Click "Delete a Google service"** or look for:
   - "Delete your Google Account" option
   - Or "More options" → "Delete your Google Account"

4. **Follow the prompts:**
   - Select what data to download (optional)
   - Confirm deletion
   - Enter password to verify
   - Click "Delete Account"

## Alternative Direct Link:

Go directly to:
https://myaccount.google.com/deleteaccount

## Important Notes:

- **Permanent**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- **Data Loss**: All data (Gmail, Drive, Photos, etc.) will be deleted
- **Download First**: Consider downloading your data first if needed
- **Timeline**: Account deletion may take a few days to complete
